### 2-D Magnetotelluric Inversion using Fully Convolutional Network (Computer Vision, Deep Learning, CNN)
_M.Tech. Thesis Project, IITR Jan. 2020 – Jun. 2020_
◦Deep learning based inversion method using convolutional neural networks (CNN) for geophysical data.
◦Input: apparent resistivity and phase for TE and TM mode at 10 stations and 17 periods; output: earth resistivity model (500×250 array).
◦Decoder-only convolutional network designed in Keras; trained on 75% of 2,425 earth models across different geologies.
◦RMSLE of 0.292 achieved on test data.
