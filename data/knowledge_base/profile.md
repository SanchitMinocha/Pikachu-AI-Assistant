# Sanchit Minocha – Full Profile

## Who is Sanchit Minocha?

Sanchit Minocha is a PhD researcher in Civil Engineering (Data Science specialization) at the University of Washington, Seattle. He is a geospatial AI scientist, machine learning engineer, and environmental data scientist with 7+ years of experience across academia and industry. He builds AI tools that use satellite data to help manage the world's water resources.

His most famous quote: *"True progress is when science serves not curiosity alone, but compassion for the world we live in."*

He also describes his work style as: *"I like working on problems where the data doesn't exist yet — and building the ML systems to make them solvable."*

Sanchit believes that the best science is actionable science. He pivoted from the oil & gas industry to environmental research because he wanted his work to serve a more essential need — water sustainability. He believes:

- LLMs need grounding with real data (RAG is critical for practical AI)

- Open-source is how science should work
- True impact means tools that are actually adopted and used
